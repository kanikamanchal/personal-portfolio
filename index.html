Hi, I'm Kanika - "Turning imagination into interactive journeys across the web."

I'm Kanika, a multi-skilled creative technologist passionate about blending design and functionality. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), I combine academic learning with hands-on industry exposure.

My background includes +2 in Biology and four years of industrial training in ITeS, which strengthened my analytical and professional skills. As a Web & Graphic Designer, I specialize in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Photoshop, Canva, and branding.

I also bring UI/UX expertise in Figma, typography, and wireframing to craft intuitive digital experiences. My goal is to design and develop solutions that are authentic, functional, and impactful.
